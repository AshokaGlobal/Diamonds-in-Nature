The Origin of the Meaning of Diamonds




Diamonds have long been one of nature’s most revered creations. Since

the dawn of humanity their beauty, strength and resilience have

enchanted people the world over. Ancient Greeks believed them to have

mystical protective powers, and it is from the Greek term ‘adamas’,

meaning invincible, that the word ‘diamond’ originates. Over time, the

magic of diamonds has continued to grow, and they have come to mark

our most cherished moments, bonds and commitments. Strong and

unbreakable, they represent timeless emotions.
